IFT Label Component

IFT (If-True-False) Label displays conditional labels based on a boolean condition. Shows different labels with visual indicators for true/false states.

Basic Usage

Simple conditional label

<sui-ift-label [condition]="true" trueLabel="Active" falseLabel="Inactive"></sui-ift-label>
