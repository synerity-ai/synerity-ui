Fieldset

Fieldset components for grouping related form elements with optional legends and collapsible functionality.

Basic Fieldset

 
<sui-fieldset legend="Basic Information">
  <div class="space-y-4">
    <div>
      <label class="block text-sm font-medium text-gray-700 mb-1">Name</label>
      <input type="text" class="w-full px-3 py-2 border border-gray-300 rounded-md" placeholder="Enter your name">
    </div>
    <div>
      <label class="block text-sm font-medium text-gray-700 mb-1">Description</label>
      <textarea class="w-full px-3 py-2 border border-gray-300 rounded-md" rows="3" placeholder="Enter description"></textarea>
    </div>
  </div>
</sui-fieldset>
