PickList

Pick list components for transferring items between two lists with filtering and selection capabilities.

Basic PickList

<sui-pick-list 
  [source]="basicSource"
  [target]="basicTarget"
  (onMoveToTarget)="onMoveToTarget($event)"
  (onMoveToSource)="onMoveToSource($event)"
  (onMoveAllToTarget)="onMoveAllToTarget($event)"
  (onMoveAllToSource)="onMoveAllToSource($event)">
</sui-pick-list>
